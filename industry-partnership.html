IIT Madras — Industry Partnership & Fellowship Program

Collaborating with top industry partners to provide funded fellowships, paid internships, and research project opportunities for outstanding students.

Fellowship & Sponsored Research

The Fellowship program provides a select cohort of students access to industry-funded research projects under joint IITM-industry mentorship. Fellows receive a monthly stipend, access to proprietary datasets and tools, and travel grants for on-site collaboration where applicable.

  • Duration: 4-6 months
  • Stipend: Competitive monthly stipend
  • Mentorship: Joint IITM faculty + Industry mentor
  • Outcome: Project report + certificate + recommendation letter

Paid Internships & Hiring Pathway

Industry partners will offer paid internship slots for students who perform well in capstone projects or through selective interview drives. Outstanding interns may be considered for full-time roles or fast-track hiring pipelines.

How to Apply / Express Interest

If you're interested in the Fellowship or internship opportunities, please fill out the expression of interest form (coming soon), or contact us at support@study.iitm.ac.in. Include "Partnership Program" in the subject line and mention your current program level and areas of interest.
