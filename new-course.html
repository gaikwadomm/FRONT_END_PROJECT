Course Overview

This 16-week, cohort-based program covers foundations of modern AI, large language models, model fine-tuning, evaluation, MLOps, and deployment. It is aimed at learners with prior programming experience (Python) and basic ML understanding. The course includes hands-on projects, mentorship from IIT faculty, and industry-facing capstone.

Syllabus (high level)

  • Week 1-2: Math & PyTorch refresher
  • Week 3-6: Deep Learning foundations (CNNs, RNNs, Transformers)
  • Week 7-9: Large Language Models & Fine-tuning (LLMs)
  • Week 10-12: MLOps & Model Serving
  • Week 13-16: Capstone project and evaluation
